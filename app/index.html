About International Space Apps Challenge

The International Space Apps Challenge is an international mass collaborative hackathon focused on space exploration that takes place over 48-hours in cities around the world. The event embraces collaborative problem solving with a goal of producing relevant open-source solutions to address global needs applicable to both life on Earth and life in space. NASA is leading this global collaboration along with a number of additional government collaborators and 100+ local partner organizations.


In 2014, Boston joins this event for the first time.


International Space Apps Boston is organized by the ISAC Boston team and Binnovative.org*.

*Binnovative represents the remote collaboration team project with International Space Apps Challenge Tokyo. If you are interested in learning more, contact info@SpaceAppsBoston.org or info@binnovative.org

Answers to your Burning Questions

When?

9:00AM 4/12/2014 -
4:00PM 4/13/2014

Where?

Cambridge Innovation Center
Coolest work space in Cambridge!

How many?

150 awesome spots + waitlist

How?

Register here for the official event!

Who can attend?

18 years or older. If you're under 18, please get permission from a responsible adult. You must register to participate.

What can I play with?

Web, desktop, mobile, and hardware projects are all welcome.

What's the theme?

This year's challenge will fall into 5 thematic areas: human space flight, technology in space, robotics, asteroids and Earth watch.

How much does this event cost?

Free!
Including breakfast, lunch, dinner and snacks. Of course caffeine is included!
You are more than welcome to purchase original t-shirts to show your support.

Want more Info?

Join us for the information session at MIT Media Lab on March 22nd.

How big should teams be? Can I do it alone?

Teams should be at most 5. Yes - you can solo, or come and make friends!

How will my product be judged??

A team of experts from tech and science will take into account creativity, ease of use, level of complexity, use of available space data, and team dynamics.

Can I watch the event online?

Yes. The event will be streamed on the internet.

Can I leave early?

You need to be an active participant on both days in order to be eligible for prizes and freebies.

Other Questions? Reach out to us!

Send us a note. We'd to hear from you.
